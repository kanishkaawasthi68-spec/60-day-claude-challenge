# 🚚 Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

An interactive Supply Chain Crisis Management Simulation built with React. In this simulation, users take on the role of a company executive and make strategic decisions to handle real-world supply chain disruptions.

## ✨ Features

- 🎲 Random company and crisis generation
- 🏢 Dynamic company profiles
- 🚨 Multiple crisis scenarios (Factory Fire, Cyberattack, Port Strike, Supplier Bankruptcy, etc.)
- ⚔️ War Room decision-making
- 🤝 Supplier negotiation simulation
- 👨‍💼 CEO Boardroom leadership challenges
- 🤖 AI strategy investment decisions
- 📊 Real-time business metrics
- 📈 Performance dashboard with final analysis
- 🔄 Different experience on every playthrough

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- Supply Chain Management
- Crisis Management
- Business Decision Making
- Negotiation Skills
- Leadership under Pressure
- AI in Supply Chain

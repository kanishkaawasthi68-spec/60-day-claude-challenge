## Summary
Implemented an AI Shark Tank Simulator where users can pitch startup ideas and receive AI-generated evaluation.

## Features
- Startup pitch form
- Multi-step workflow
- AI judges introduction
- AI-generated Q&A
- Judge reactions
- Startup scoring
- Final investment decision
- Leaderboard
- Download report
- Share result
- Responsive UI
- Loading and toast notifications
- Confetti animation

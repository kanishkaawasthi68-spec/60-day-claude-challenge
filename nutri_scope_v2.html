About NutriScope

NutriScope v2 is a client-side nutrition tracker built for educational awareness. All data is stored locally in your browser — nothing is sent to any server. The food database covers 60+ common Indian foods with macronutrients and 10 micronutrients per item.

Nutrient targets are calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor BMR equation combined with ICMR and WHO Recommended Dietary Allowances for adults. These are general population averages and individual needs may differ significantly.

For clinical nutrition assessment, consult a Registered Dietitian (RD) or a licensed physician.
